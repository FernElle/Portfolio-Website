About Me

Hey there! I'm Luzaan, a game development student and artist passionate about crafting immersive worlds, quirky characters, and clever gameplay. Whether it's coding a riddle game or designing an otherworldly environment, I'm always chasing creativity and a bit of controlled chaos.

I specialize in 2.5D puzzle platformers, storytelling with mythological flair, and games that make people feel something (preferably awe, not rage... unless it's intentional).

Tools I use: Unity, C#, Photoshop, pen & paper, and a LOT of coffee.
